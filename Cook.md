SECTION 3 — FORMULA (10g BATCH)

- 3.5 g L-Theanine
- 2.5 g Caffeine Citrate
- 2.0 g L-Tyrosine
- 1.0 g Theobromine
- 1.0 g MSM

Total: 10 g solids

----------------------------------------------
SECTION 4 — PROFESSIONAL CRYSTAL PROCESS

1. Add all powders into glass beaker

2. Add ~10–15 mL HOT distilled water
   → Just enough to fully dissolve

3. Stir continuously until solution is CLEAR
   → No particles = better crystals

4. FILTER solution through paper filter
   → Removes impurities = clarity

5. Transfer to clean glass dish or jar

6. Cover loosely (dust protection)

7. Leave undisturbed:
   - 48–96 hours
   - Stable room temperature
   - No vibration

8. Allow slow evaporation

9. Once fully dry:
   - Remove crystal mass
   - Break into shards or keep clusters
